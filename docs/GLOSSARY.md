# Glossary

## Terms

- **Vault**: A savings container with optional time lock and goal
- **Time Lock**: Prevents withdrawal until specific timestamp
- **Goal Amount**: Target savings amount to reach
- **Protocol Fee**: 0.5% fee charged on deposits
- **Metadata**: User-defined name/description for vault
- **BPS**: Basis Points (100 = 1%)
- **Base**: Layer 2 Ethereum scaling solution
